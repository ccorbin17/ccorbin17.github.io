Future Career Goals

"A marketing technologist is a technically skilled person who designs and operates technology solutions in the service of marketing. This isn’t just about embedding IT services within marketing, though. Good marketing technologists strive to understand the context of the technology. They’re passionate about reimagining what marketing can do in a digital world."
-Scott Brinker
A marketing technologist is a fairly new position and often referred to as a "unicorn" profession because of the few people who choose to pursue it. I want to be one of these unicorns. I believe that marketing technologists in the United States will need to appeal to foreign markets in order to make brands more international. Countries like China and South Korea, my concentrations in culture study, are great consumers that with the right advertising could find that United States products can work for them. As I learn more about how technology works and these two East Asian cultures, I hope to become the marketing technologist that businesses need in the near future
